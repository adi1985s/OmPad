      OmPad

      OmPad is a notepad to store notes, texts or even poems. It is focused in simplicity and speed for quick note taking while inspiration is at its peak.

      • Themes: Choose the perfect themes for your situation (top left)
      • 5 Pages: Use them to save different content (bottom left)
      • Saves as you type: If you close the browser it will still be there

      OmPad is build in HTML5/jQuery and it is a great example on how modern websites can be so tiny yet so functional. You can create your own themes very easily. You can also use OmPad while being offline.
